Lin-guage

My capstone project. This program allows users to learn about, or even create their own, languages. It utilizes a custom built and researched Ruby on Rails database. The website itself has three main functionalities: Explore, Compare, and Create. "Explore" displays infomration for each language. "Compare" has a custom algorithm to rank language pairs by similarity. "Create" allows users to generate their own unique language by "mixing" two others together.

Don't Drink The Punch

This project was completed for a Hackathon. It was completed with a group of 3 other students, within a total of about 6 hours of work. The game works by flipping over cards and trying to acrue as many points as possible(as shown on each card face) before flipping over too many "Punch" cards(which register as -1 life). Users can also find extra life cards.
